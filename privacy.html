Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2026  ·  App: GameVault for Android  ·  Developer: Team GameVault

Overview

GameVault ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of the GameVault Android application.

By using GameVault, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use GameVault:

Data Type Purpose Stored?
Email address Account creation and authentication Yes
Display name & username Public profile and social features Yes
Game ratings & written reviews App activity, community feed Yes
Favorites list Personal game log and recommendations Yes
Optional profile fields (bio, Discord, Twitch, genres) Profile personalization Yes (only if provided)
Camera / photo library access Profile picture upload only Not stored
Camera Access: GameVault does not store camera data. Camera and photo library access is used solely to allow you to select or capture a profile picture. The image you choose is uploaded to your account; raw camera data is never recorded, retained, or transmitted beyond that single upload action.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your GameVault account
  • To display your profile, reviews, and favorites within the app
  • To power the global and following community feeds
  • To provide AI-powered game recommendations based on your activity
  • To respond to support requests sent to gamevault@uri.edu

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the GameVault service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. The only exceptions are:

  • Firebase (Google): GameVault uses Google Firebase for authentication, database storage, and file hosting. Data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • IGDB (Twitch): Game metadata (titles, cover art, descriptions) is fetched from the IGDB API. No personal data is sent to IGDB.
  • Public reviews: Reviews and ratings you post are visible to all GameVault users by design.

Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS
  • Data at rest is encrypted via Firebase's default encryption
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication — we do not store passwords

While we implement industry-standard safeguards, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at gamevault@uri.edu. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Public reviews you have posted may remain visible briefly after account deletion while caches clear.

Children's Privacy

GameVault is rated Everyone and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of GameVault after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
