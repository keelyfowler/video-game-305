Business Strategy

Lean Canvas

Product: GameVault ~ Video Game Tracker/Reviewer
Team: 305Soft — Keely, Liam, Nate, Bianca
Date: 04/27/26
Problem
  • No clean, social-focused app for tracking and reviewing video games the way Letterboxd does for movies.
  • Gamers struggle to track what they've played, want to play, or would recommend.
  • Reviews are scattered across Reddit, YouTube, Metacritic, and Steam — no centralized personal hub.
Solution
  • Personal game library tracker: Played, Playing, and Want to Play lists.
  • Social reviews with star ratings, written comments, and recommendations.
  • Personalized feed based on user ratings and favorite genres.
Unique Value Proposition
The social hub for gamers to track, review, and discover their next favorite game.
A clean, community-driven platform where gamers build their personal game journal, share opinions, and discover new games through friends.
Just like Letterboxd — built for gaming culture.
Unfair Advantage
  • Built specifically for gamers — not a general review site.
  • Social-first design with friend activity feeds and a clean modern UI (unlike cluttered Metacritic).
  • Potential gaming platform integration.
  • Strong natural engagement — gamers love ranking and sharing opinions.
Customer Segments
Primary
  • Casual gamers, ages 16–24
  • Hardcore / competitive gamers, 18–30
  • College students, 18–22
  • Console & PC players
Key Metrics
  • Registered users & monthly active users
  • Games logged & reviews written
  • Friend connections made
  • User retention rate
  • App store rating & review sentiment
Channels
  • App Store & Google Play listings
  • TikTok, Instagram, Reddit gaming communities
  • Partnerships with YouTubers & Twitch streamers
  • Word of mouth via college gaming communities
  • Letterboxd user crossover
Early Adopters
  • Gaming content creators on YouTube & Twitch
  • Reddit gaming community members
  • Existing Letterboxd users
Cost Structure
  • FlutterFlow development & hosting fees
  • Firebase backend costs (scales with user base)
  • IGDB API access — free via Twitch developer program
Revenue Streams
Free Tier
  • Basic game tracking & reviews
  • Capped at following 5 users
GameVault Pro — $4.99/mo
  • Unlimited follows
  • Detailed stats & custom lists
  • Early access to features
  • Managed via RevenueCat
Advertising
  • Google AdMob banner ads on profile pages for free-tier users
