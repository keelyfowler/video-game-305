About the App

Built for Gamers

GameVault is a Letterboxd-style mobile app built specifically for video game enthusiasts. Whether you want to log every game you've ever played, share your hot takes with the community, or discover your next favorite title, GameVault is the place to do it.

With a clean, dark interface designed for gamers, real-time community reviews, and IGDB-powered game data, GameVault puts everything you need in one place.
